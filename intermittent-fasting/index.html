Intermittent Fasting

The easiest and quickest way to lose weight

I have struggled with weight as far back as I can remember. I use to think I was just big boned, had bad genetics, was short and just about everything else you can imagine. I’ve read books, tried a bunch off different diets and fads to see some benefits but gain it all back (and sometimes more).

It’s been a coaster of ups and downs and when I was just about to give up, I found intermittent fasting.

It’s not what you eat but when you eat!

So simple! The only concept is to limit your calories by limiting the amount of time you’re allowed to eat. There’s a lot of varieties of intermittent fasting but the simplest I found was
16/8.

During 16/8, you eat for 8 hours and fast for 16 hours. You can choose whatever time frame works bests for your life as long as you can keep to it.

I choose to eat during the hours of 12 pm to 8 pm. All other times, I didn’t eat anything other than non-calorie drinks, coffee, and lots of water.

That’s it! It’s that simple. There’s nothing to buy and nothing else to do. You can choose to exercise but it’s not required. It’ll speed up your weight loss but at the end of the day, in order to lose weight, you have to eat less than you burn.

weight loss = calories_in < calories_out
